Procedure for On-Off Control experiment:

In the User Interface (UI) provided for this experiment:


Instructions:-

  1. Enter the value of the controller hysteresis in its respective control box.
  1. Let the DEFAULT values of Random Parameters are as it is. DO NOT enter your values.
  1. After that, press the RUN button given at the top.
  1. Then calculate the various parameters by observing the graph and enter the values in their respective control box.
  1. Press the check button. And Repeat steps 1-4 for different values of controller hysteresis.
  1. If you are satisfied with the answers press the submit button to log your answers in a file.
