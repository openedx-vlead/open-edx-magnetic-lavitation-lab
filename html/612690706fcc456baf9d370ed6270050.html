Procedure:

In the User Interface (UI) provided for this experiment:


Instructions:-

  1. You will get the random values in Random Parameter a, and Random Parameter b Indicators.
  1. According to the given data calculate Plant gain (DC Gain), Damping Factor , & Natural Frequency , for each set of values.
  1. Then calculate the Rise time , Settling time , & Peak Overshoot (PO in %) values for each set of values.
  1. Enter the answer calculated above in the particular box.
  1. Check the answer for every set of values by clicking on the check button provided at the right hand side.
  1. If you are satisfied with the answers click on the submit button to log your answers in an Excel file.
