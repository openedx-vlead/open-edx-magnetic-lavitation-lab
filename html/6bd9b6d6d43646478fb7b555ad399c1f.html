Things to think about

  1. What two values does the control effort always take?
  2. Why does the actual height overshoot or undershoot the setpoint?
  3. Initially, before the height change is made, why is the controller output at its OFF value?
  4. How do the oscillations (frequency and amplitude) in actual height response vary with the controller hysteresis?
  5. How does the control effort (frequency and amplitude) vary with controller hysteresis?
  6. How does the magnitude of deviations around the reference height vary with the controller hysteresis?
